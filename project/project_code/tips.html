Tips

Planning and organization

  • Create a meal plan for the week (example )
  • Buy groceries needed in advance

Sustainability

  • Try to buy local foods
  • Add not so fresh groceries to the soup, you've forgotten in the fridge
  • Avoid plastic, you have more control on the aliments and help environment

Cooking tips

  • Use lemon slices when grilling fish: it's tastier and saves time when cleaning
  • Always have soup at the refrigerator: it's always a ready-to-eat meal
  • Have pre-cooked beans and lentils at the refrigerator: always ready for rice, pasta or whatever you'll need
  • Portions of dry fruits, cheese or a different seasoning will make your saladas tastier and will definitely catch your eye
