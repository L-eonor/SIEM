Breakfast

Oatmeal with Kiwi and sesame seeds

3 mins 🕒

Ingredients

  • 200 ml vegetal milk
  • 1 big kiwi or 2 small ones
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds

Preparation

  • Warm the milk to desired temperature
  • Cut the kiwi into cubes
  • Add the fruit to the milk bowl
  • Add sesame seeds previously toasted
